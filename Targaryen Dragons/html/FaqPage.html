Frequently Asked Questions

Account Creation:

Q: Do I need to create an account to shop at Mary's Toys?
A: Yes, to make purchases and access exclusive offers, account creation is required. It's a quick and easy process that allows you to track orders, save your preferences, and streamline future purchases.

Q: How can I create an account?
A: Simply click on the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button located on the top right corner of our website. Follow the prompts to enter your details such as name, email address, and preferred password. Once completed, you'll have your own Mary's Toys account.

Shopping:

Q: What kinds of environmentally-friendly toys does Mary's Toys offer?
A: Mary's Toys offers a wide range of eco-friendly toys made from sustainable materials such as recycled wood, bamboo, and more. Our products promote creativity, sustainability, and environmental consciousness.

Q: How can I find specific toys on your website?
A: You can use our search bar located at the top of the page to find toys by name, category, or brand. Additionally, you can browse through our curated collections and categories to discover new and exciting environmentally-friendly toys.

Shipping:

Q: How are packages sent to customers?
A: At Mary's Toys, we prioritize eco-friendly packaging solutions. Your orders will be carefully packaged using recycled and recyclable materials whenever possible. We aim to minimize waste and reduce our carbon footprint throughout the shipping process.

Q: What shipping options are available?
A: We offer standard and expedited shipping options to accommodate your needs. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location and the selected shipping method. You can view shipping details and estimated delivery times during the checkout process.

Customer Support:

Q: How can I contact Mary's Toys for further assistance?
A: We're here to help! If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team. You can contact us via email at support@marystoys.com or by filling out the contact form on our website. Our dedicated team will respond to your inquiries promptly and provide the assistance you need.

Q: What are your customer support hours?
A: Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (EST). We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during our regular business hours. Any messages received outside of these hours will be addressed promptly on the following business day.

We hope this FAQ answers any questions you may have.
If you need further assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to us.
