Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Mary's Toys. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return items in their original condition within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Conditions for Return

  • Items must be returned in their original packaging.
  • Returns must include all accessories, manuals, and any other items that came with the product.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • Returns are subject to inspection upon arrival. Any item that is damaged, not in its original condition, or missing parts may be ineligible for a refund.

How to Return

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at customerservice@marystoys.com or call us at 1-800-123-4567. We will provide you with instructions on how to return your items.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If your return is approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.

Have a Complaint?

If you have any complaints or concerns, please fill out our complaints form and we will address your issue promptly.
