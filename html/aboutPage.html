About Our Company

Our Mission:

To create long-lasting, high-quality, sustainable toys that encourage imaginative play for children of all ethnicities, backgrounds, and demographics!

Our Backstory:

Mary's Toys was created in honor of Sarah Johnson's late child, Mary, who loved toys and playing outdoors. With the help of her close college friends, Sarah channeled her grief into the development of a meaningful toy brand with diversity, sustainability, and imagination at its core. Combining the many talents of her team and their diverse backgounds, the company has been able to grow and find global success in the competitive toy industry. To learn more about our company's hard-working leadership team, and the unique backgrounds they bring to the company, view the "Our Leadership" tab on the top of the page.

Our Company Values:

  • Sustainability - our toys are made with sustainable materials, such as bamboo and recycled materials that are locally sourced.
  • Diversity- our toys are created to appeal to children of different ethnicities, backgrounds, and demographics, sustainable fun is for everyone!
  • Craftsmanship - our toys are hand-crafted to ensure the highest quality product (these toys will out live you)!
  • Imagination - we build toys that encourage imaginative play with resuable components and gender neutral themes.
  • Satisfaction - we strive to create toys that you and your child will love. Not satisfied? Connect with one of our friendly customer service representatives for a hassle-free return. Your satisfaction is important to us!
