Our Leadership Team

Sarah Johnson

Born with an innate desire to spark joy and imagination, Sarah Johnson found solace in creating alongside her college friends. When her daughter, Mary, passed away, Sarah sought to channel her grief into something meaningful. Together, they founded "Mary's Toys," a children's toy company dedicated to sustainability and imaginative play. Inspired by Mary's playful spirit, they crafted eco-friendly toys from recycled materials, nurturing childhood wonder while minimizing environmental impact. Sarah's unwavering dedication and her friends' skillsets propelled Mary's Toys to success, earning recognition and delighting children worldwide. With each whimsical creation, Sarah keeps Mary's memory alive, proving that love, friendship, and sustainability can build a legacy that inspires smiles and laughter.

Mei Ling

Mei Ling, born and raised in bustling Hong Kong, always dreamed of making a difference. After witnessing mountains of plastic toys discarded in her city, she teamed up with college friends from diverse backgrounds and together, they founded "Mary's Toys," crafting adorable, eco-friendly toys from recycled materials. Inspired by local folklore and childhood memories, her creations sparked imagination while teaching sustainability. Mei's sharp business acumen and dedication to her community propelled Mary's Toys to success, winning awards and earning the hearts of children worldwide. Now, with her friends by her side, Mei proves that sustainable fun and social impact can blossom from a single seed of inspiration.

Maya Patel

Born amidst the vibrant textile traditions of Gujarat, Maya Patel always held a love for creating with purpose. At university, her frustration with cheap, plastic toys sparked a conversation with friends. Together, they launched "Mary's Toys," crafting beautiful, educational toys from upcycled fabrics and natural materials. Inspired by Indian mythology and folk stories, she aims to make toys that nurture cultural pride while promoting sustainability. Maya's entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to her community propelled the company into a global success, garnering recognition and igniting children's imaginations worldwide. With her friends by her side, Maya proves that cultural heritage and environmental responsibility can weave together to create playful magic.

Ayesha Johnson

Ayesha Johnson, raised in the heart of Brooklyn, always held a fire for social justice and artistic expression. Witnessing limited representation in mainstream toys fuelled her college dorm room discussions with friends. From these conversations sprouted "Mary's Toys," crafting diverse, eco-conscious toys from recycled materials. Drawing inspiration from African folktales and contemporary heroes, her creations empowered young minds while fostering environmental awareness. Ayesha's business savvy and dedication to her community propelled the company to acclaim, winning awards and igniting imaginations globally. With her friends by her side, Ayesha proves that inclusivity, sustainability, and playful adventure can weave together a powerful legacy.
